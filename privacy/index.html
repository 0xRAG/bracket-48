Bracket 48

Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 6, 2026

Bracket 48 is an independent, unofficial app for entertainment with friends. It is not affiliated with or endorsed by FIFA, tournament organizers, teams, or federations. Bracket 48 does not offer cash rewards, prizes, betting, or gambling.

Information We Collect

  • Account information from Sign in with Apple, such as your Apple-provided user identifier and email address if Apple provides it.
  • Your display name.
  • Your bracket picks, group names, group memberships, invite codes, and saved entries.
  • Basic technical information needed to operate the service, such as authentication sessions and server request metadata.

How We Use Information

  • To sign you in and maintain your account.
  • To save brackets, groups, invite links, and leaderboard entries.
  • To operate, secure, debug, and improve the app.

Tracking And Advertising

Bracket 48 does not use your data for third-party advertising, does not sell personal information, and does not track you across other companies' apps or websites.

Sharing

Information you intentionally share with a group, such as your display name, bracket entries, and scores, may be visible to other members of that group. We may use service providers, such as hosting and authentication providers, to operate the app.

Account Deletion

You can delete your account in the app from Profile > Delete Account. Deletion removes your account and associated app data, including brackets, groups you own, and saved entries, subject to any backups or logs retained for security and legal purposes.

Children

Bracket 48 is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child provided personal information, contact us so we can delete it.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact support@bracket48.app.

Changes

We may update this policy as the app changes. Material updates will be reflected on this page.
